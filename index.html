About Us

Northern Uganda Farmer’s Limited Company is a leading agricultural organization committed to equipping farmers with modern farming techniques, improving food security, and fostering sustainable agribusiness. We work closely with smallholder and commercial farmers, providing them with the skills, knowledge, and resources needed to maximize productivity and profitability.

Our Areas of Work

We specialize in training and capacity building, offering expert guidance on crop production, livestock management, agribusiness development, and climate-smart agriculture. Our services extend to soil fertility management, mechanized farming, irrigation solutions, post-harvest handling, and market linkage support. We also facilitate access to quality farm inputs and promote environmentally friendly farming practices that enhance sustainability and productivity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform agriculture in Northern Uganda by empowering farmers with the best farming skills and knowledge. We are dedicated to increasing agricultural productivity, promoting sustainable farming methods, and strengthening market access for farmers to improve livelihoods and food security in the region.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving and self-sufficient farming community in Northern Uganda, where agriculture is a key driver of economic growth, sustainability, and prosperity. Through innovation, inclusivity, and strategic partnerships, we aim to make farming more efficient, profitable, and resilient to climate change.

Our Objectives

  1. Promote sustainable rice farming practices
  2. Facilitate market access for members
  3. Provide training and capacity building
  4. Advocate for farmers' rights
  5. Enhance value addition processes

Collaboration and Partnerships

We work hand in hand with government agencies, NGOs, research institutions, financial organizations, and private sector players to enhance agricultural development. Our collaborations help farmers access funding, training, modern technology, and sustainable agricultural solutions. By connecting farmers with markets and investors, we ensure that agriculture remains a viable and profitable sector for rural communities.

Our Dedication to Farmers

At Northern Uganda Farmer’s Limited Company, we are passionate about empowering farmers. We believe that knowledge, technology, and innovation are key to transforming agriculture. Our team is committed to supporting farmers at every stage—from seed selection to post-harvest handling—ensuring they achieve the best possible outcomes. Through continuous learning, mentorship, and advocacy, we remain dedicated to building a stronger and more resilient agricultural sector.
